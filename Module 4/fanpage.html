Tribute to Linken Park!

Biography

Linken Park is an American rock band from Agoura Hills, California. Formed in 1996, Linken Park rose to fame through their debut album Hybrid Theory. Subsequently releasing Meteora and Minutes to Midnight which continued to enjoy success and top billboards. Throughout the years, Linken Park has explored a wide variety of musical types and is one of the best selling bands of the 21st century. Due to the death of longtime lead singer Chester Bennington in 2017, Linken Park has been on indefinte hiatus leaving the bands future in uncertainty.
